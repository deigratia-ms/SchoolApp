Our Curriculum Approach

Each child progresses at their own pace, following their natural curiosity and interests while being guided by trained Montessori teachers.

Children learn through manipulation of specially designed Montessori materials that make abstract concepts concrete and understandable.

Subjects are not taught in isolation but are interconnected, helping children understand the relationships between different areas of study.

Assessment & Progress

We believe in assessing children's progress in a way that respects their individual development and maintains the joy of learning.

Observation Based

Teachers maintain detailed records of each child's activities and progress through careful daily observation.

Regular Communication

Parent-teacher conferences are held regularly to discuss your child's development and achievements.

Portfolio Development

Each child maintains a portfolio of work that demonstrates their progress and achievements.

Progress Reports

Detailed progress reports are provided twice a year, highlighting your child's growth in all areas.
